What we do

We provide remote scenario based assessment, where clinical instructors can design their own clinical scenarios, disseminate to ther students, and receive specific automated feeback on the student's response at each stage of the scenario.

Scalable

Train many students at once, without the need for one-to-one practical coaching.

Support for any device

Supports both on desktop and mobile, so your students can learn on the go.

Data Driven
